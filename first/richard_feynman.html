Richard Feynman

Richard Phillips Feynman was an American theoretical physicist, known for his work in
the path integral formulation of quantum mechanics, the theory of quantum electrodynamics,
the physics of the superfluidity of supercooled liquid helium, as well as his work in
particle physics for which he proposed the parton model.
